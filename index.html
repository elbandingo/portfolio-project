About Adam

Hello there! Thank you for stumbling upon my page to check out some of the fun, interesting things I have learned during my time at the University of Toronto Bootcamp! I came from a background in tech, having worked for a canadian bank helping clients integrate complex banking products with their business. Having spent a fair amount of time working with Corporate, Commercial, and Small Businesses, I have quickly adopted a passion for coding and building products. My goal with this website is to showcase what I have learned, and show that I can be an asset to you and your team! Please take a look below at some of the fun, interesting projects I have developed over the last 6 months!
